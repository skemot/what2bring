How it works

Simple for you. Simple for them.

Three steps for the organiser. One step for guests. Everyone stays in the loop.

1
📝
Create your event
Add a name, date, location and the list of items you need people to bring. Takes 2 minutes.
2
📲
Share the QR code
Print it, put it in a group chat, pin it to a noticeboard — your guests scan with any phone camera.
3
✅
Guests pick their item
No app, no login. Guests type their name, tap what they'll bring, and they're done. Optional email for reminders.
4
📊
You stay in control
Get notified instantly. See a live report of what's covered and what's still needed. Export it any time.
